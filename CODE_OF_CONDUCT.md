# 📜 Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making this project a welcoming and respectful space for everyone — regardless of experience level, background, or nationality.

---

## ✅ Expected Behavior

- Be kind and respectful in all interactions
- Give constructive feedback, not personal attacks
- Accept different opinions and approaches
- Help beginners — everyone starts somewhere
- Credit others for their contributions

---

## ❌ Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or hate speech
- Sharing others' private information
- Spam, self-promotion in issues/PRs
- Using this project for illegal activities
- Violating WhatsApp's Terms of Service
