## 🛡️ Security Best Practices for Users

To keep your bot secure:

1. **Never share your `.env` file** — it contains your MongoDB URI and owner number
2. **Never share your session files** — anyone with your session can control your WhatsApp
3. **Set MODE to `private`** in `setting.js` if you don't want public access
4. **Keep dependencies updated** — run `npm update` regularly
5. **Use a strong MongoDB password** — do not use simple passwords
6. **Don't run the bot as root** on Linux servers
